Some of Elvis Presley's earliest recordings - including takes of All Shook Up - are going under the hammer on Sunday at a Los Angeles auction. Highlights of the two hour-long collection will get their public debut at Bonhams auction house on Saturday. The "pre-masters" include a take of Jailhouse Rock, religious songs, material for his first Christmas album, and banter between Presley, members of his band and engineer Thorne Nogar. "We've had them for a lot of years, and I think the people should enjoy them," Nogar's son Stephen, 57, said.
